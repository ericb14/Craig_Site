Craig Mannion
Health Coach and Wellness Advocate



I was born in Florida, however I spent my childhood and graduated high school in northern Georgia. After graduating high school I returned to Florida and earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology from Stetson University. During my undergraduate work, I also travelled and studied in the Amazon Rainforest, the Florida Keys, skydived, and received my Open Water SCUBA certification, all within a year! Since, then I have realized that it is better to be more grounded, often desiring a float-like state, which is why I love the ocean and observing the beauty of life that lies beneath. From experience, I learned to study and appreciate, but more importantly to protect our precious marine ecosystems.
